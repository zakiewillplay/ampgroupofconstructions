About Us

Building Dreams Into Reality

Established in Srinagar, AMP Group of Constructions has been delivering excellence since 2015.

Founder: Aabid Mushtaq

Founded by Aabid Mushtaq

AMP Group of Constructions was founded with a singular vision: to bring world-class construction standards to Jammu & Kashmir. Under the leadership of Aabid Mushtaq, the firm has grown from a small contracting business to one of Srinagar's most trusted names in construction and design.

With over 11 years of hands-on experience in the construction industry, Aabid has personally overseen every project, ensuring that each one meets the highest standards of quality, safety, and aesthetic excellence.

Our philosophy is simple — "Building Dreams Into Reality". We don't just construct buildings; we create spaces where families grow, businesses thrive, and communities flourish.

We are a full-service construction and design firm, offering complete solutions from concept to completion. With over 200 successfully delivered projects across both residential and commercial sectors, we bring expertise, innovation, and quality to every project. Our dedicated team works closely with clients to transform their vision into reality, ensuring excellence at every stage of the construction and design process.
